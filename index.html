About this calculator

Computes a fitted regression: y = 7.807852e-10 x⁴ + (-1.799129e-3) x³ + 8.971645e-4 x² + 0.4413457 x + 16.40816 where x is altitude in meters and y is airspeed in km/h. Landing burn begins at ~800 m.

Simple estimator — not an official SpaceX source.
